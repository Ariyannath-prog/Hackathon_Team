Renewable Energy Tycoon

How to Play

  • Objective: Build a city by placing energy sources and growing your population to 100 while keeping pollution low.
  • Energy Sources: Choose between renewable (cost: 50, low pollution) or non-renewable (cost: 30, high pollution) energy sources.
  • Gameplay: Click a button to select an energy type, then click an empty grid cell to place it. Use "Next Turn" to progress.
  • Money: Earn money based on population growth. Spend it to build energy sources.
  • Pollution: Non-renewable sources increase pollution. High pollution (>100) ends the game in failure.
  • Endings: Reach 100 population with low pollution for a green utopia, moderate pollution for a balanced city, or fail by pollution or time (20 turns).
